Brenda Cockerham

80 acres of peace on Route 580 · Oil Springs, Kentucky

Welcome to the holler

After 30+ years with the Johnson County Extension Office and raising a family in these hills, Brenda finally hung up the office keys and came home to the land she loves. These days you’ll find her tending the big garden, feeding the hummingbirds, keeping the bees, and watching grandkids run wild on the same creeks she played in as a girl.

Retirement looks like early-morning coffee on the porch, canning whatever the garden gives, and waving at neighbors on four-wheelers headed to Paintsville Lake.
